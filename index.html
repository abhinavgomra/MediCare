The Opportunity

A Digital Health Revolution in India

The Indian healthcare market is at a digital tipping point. With over 500 million smartphone users and rising expectations for digital convenience, the opportunity to disrupt the traditional, inefficient model has never been greater.

Our focus on the immediate, high-frequency needs of urban and semi-urban patients positions us perfectly to capture a significant share of this booming market.

Business Model

A Diversified & Scalable Revenue Strategy

Transaction Fees (B2B)

A small commission from clinics for each successful appointment booked via our platform.

Subscription Fees (B2B)

Premium listings for pharmacies & hospitals offering enhanced visibility and analytics.

Premium Features (B2C)

A user subscription for advanced features like family accounts and insurance integration.

Data Insights (Future)

Anonymized data on health trends for pharmaceutical and research organizations.

Competitive Advantage

Why We Win: The Power of Integration

While competitors focus on a single vertical like medicine delivery or doctor booking, our key differentiator is integrating the entire patient journey. This creates a stickier product, builds stronger user habits, and results in higher lifetime value.
